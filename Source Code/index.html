Frequently Asked Questions

Google Gemini is a family of multimodal large language models developed by Google AI, designed to understand and operate across various forms of information, including text, images, audio, and video.

Gemini is distinguished by its native multimodality, meaning it was trained from the ground up to understand and operate across different types of data, rather than having separate components for each.

Gemini is being rolled out in phases. Access is currently available through various Google products and APIs, with more widespread availability planned.

Google is committed to responsible AI development and stringent privacy standards. Data used by Gemini is handled according to Google's privacy policy.
